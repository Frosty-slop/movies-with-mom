THE MISSION

You know the feeling. You're watching a movie with your mom and suddenly there's a scene that has everyone reaching for their phones. Maybe it's an unexpected sex scene. Maybe it's graphic violence that comes out of nowhere. Maybe it's crude humor that makes the room go quiet.

Movies with Mom exists to fix that. We review films and TV shows specifically for the mother-child viewing experience; flagging the moments that make things awkward so you can make an informed choice before you press play.

It's not about avoiding anything challenging or edgy. Good stories explore difficult territory. It's about having the information up front so you can decide what's right for your movie night; and walk in prepared.

HOW IT WORKS
  • Search any movie or TV show from the homepage to pull up details, ratings, and a mom-friendliness analysis.
  • The Mom Meter gives a quick 1–5 heart rating for how comfortable the viewing experience is likely to be.
  • Before You Press Play flags specific content concerns (violence, language, nudity, awkward humor) with plain-language descriptions.
  • Use Mom Safe Search to get AI-powered recommendations filtered by mood, era, and type; all vetted for the mom-friendly experience.
